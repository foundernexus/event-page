About this session

Most pitch deck advice is generic. This isn't.

For one hour, you'll watch a seasoned VC open a real founder's actual deck and react the way he would in a real partner meeting. No hypotheticals, no polished "after" version — an honest, live teardown of a deck that's out in the world right now.

60 minutes · live teardown followed by open Q&A
What you'll walk away with
A live, slide-by-slide review of a real seed-stage deck
What a VC actually looks for — and what makes them lose interest
The line between a slide that works and one that raises questions
Open Q&A — bring your own deck and fundraising questions

How our rooms work

Organized by stage and decision, not geography

FounderNexus rooms put you with founders carrying the same decision you are. This session is built for Stage 1 and Stage 2 — founders who are raising or sharpening their story.
